The Plan

90 Days to a Working Sales Engine

ARAGON
Phase 1: Days 1-30
Define, Position, Launch
✓ Define ICP across three segments. AI-native startups building agent products. Enterprise AI teams adding agent capabilities to existing platforms. SaaS companies embedding integrations into their products. Each segment has different buyers, different pain, and different deal structures. Map all three.
✓ Build the enterprise sales process. Establish qualification criteria, demo flow, technical evaluation framework, and procurement path. StackOne sells to technical buyers who evaluate differently than business buyers. The process has to match.
✓ Develop competitive positioning against Merge. Merge is the incumbent. Every deal will involve a Merge comparison. Build the battle card: where StackOne wins on AI-agent infrastructure, MCP servers, tool-calling, and security. Make the differentiation concrete and provable.
✓ Launch outbound to US enterprise AI teams and SaaS platforms. Build target lists, write sequences, start conversations. Lead with the AI-agent use case. "How are your agents connecting to enterprise systems today?" is the opening question.
Phase 2: Days 31-60
Work Deals, Close Contracts
✓ Work live deals through the full cycle. Run discovery, coordinate technical evaluations with engineering, build business cases, and navigate procurement. Push qualified opportunities to close.
✓ Close first enterprise contracts. Land the initial US enterprise deals that validate the sales motion and prove the market. Each closed deal becomes a reference and a case study for the next ten.
✓ Build the demo-to-close playbook. Document every step from first demo to signed contract. What works in discovery, what objections come up, how technical evaluations run, what makes procurement move. Repeatable and transferable.
✓ Establish forecast discipline. Implement plan letter methodology for every qualified deal. Stage-based pipeline with clear criteria for advancement. Weekly forecast reviews with Romain and the team so pipeline is never a mystery.
Phase 3: Days 61-90
Scale Pipeline, Structure for Growth
✓ Drive pipeline to $5M+. Qualified pipeline across all three segments. Enough deal volume to validate which segments close fastest, which have the highest ACV, and where to concentrate resources.
✓ Define the hiring plan for AEs. Based on 90 days of real selling data, define what the US sales team needs to look like: how many reps, what profiles, which segments they own, comp structure, and ramp expectations.
✓ Build the partnership channel. AI agent companies, system integrators, and technology partners who can drive co-sell and referral pipeline. Identify the partnerships that accelerate revenue beyond direct outbound.
✓ Present the 12-month revenue plan. Revenue targets by segment, headcount plan, channel mix, and investment requirements. The roadmap from founding GTM hire to a scaled US sales organization.
