The Plan

90 Days to a Working Sales Engine

JEFF
ARAGON
Phase 1: Days 1-30
Assess, Align, and Start Closing
✓ Ride along and diagnose. Sit in on your AE's active deals. Understand the current sales motion, where deals stall, what objections come up, and what proof points land. Map the real buying process from the AE's lived experience.
✓ Carry deals alongside the AE. Take on pipeline directly while coaching in real time. I do not teach theory. I sell next to your AE so they see how discovery, negotiation, and close actually work in live deals with staffing agency buyers.
✓ ICP refinement. Not all agencies are the same buyer. Segment by vertical (healthcare vs. IT vs. light industrial), size (100 placements/month vs. 1000), ATS platform, and AI readiness. Define who buys fast, who needs more proof, and where your AE should spend time.
✓ Build the sales process. Document the repeatable motion: discovery framework, qualification criteria, demo structure, objection handling, pricing conversation, and close process. Give your AE a system that works, not a pep talk.
Phase 2: Days 31-60
Build the Engine - Outbound, Events, Partnerships
✓ Outbound at scale. Design and launch targeted outbound campaigns to staffing agency executives. Email sequences, LinkedIn, phone. Test messaging across verticals. Build the repeatable pipeline generation system.
✓ Event and trade show strategy. Asendia already exhibits at TechServe Alliance, Staffing World, Executive Forum, and Collaboration X. Build the event playbook: pre-event outreach, booth strategy, post-event follow-up sequence. Every event should generate 20+ qualified conversations.
✓ ATS partnerships. Build co-marketing and integration relationships with Bullhorn, JobDiva, Avionté, and Tracker. These partnerships are distribution channels. An agency using Bullhorn who hears "Asendia integrates natively" is 3x more likely to take a meeting.
✓ Case studies and content. Turn early customer wins into case studies with real numbers: screens automated, time saved, cost reduced, placements accelerated. Publish across LinkedIn, staffing industry publications, and the website. Content that positions AI as a revenue multiplier, not a recruiter replacement.
Phase 3: Days 61-90
Scale the Motion, Grow the Team
✓ Revenue scaling. Multiple agencies closing per month. Pipeline predictable. The 3-week sales cycle is repeatable and documented. Your AE is operating at a level they could not have reached alone because the system around them is working.
✓ CRM and reporting infrastructure. Clean pipeline, accurate forecasting, conversion metrics by segment and channel. The data tells us exactly where to invest next and when it is time to hire the second AE.
✓ Hiring plan defined. Based on 90 days of real deal data, define the next sales hire: role, profile, comp plan, ramp expectations. Build the onboarding system so new reps get productive in weeks, not months. Your AE becomes the model for what good looks like.
✓ Product feedback shaping roadmap. Customer insights feeding directly into product development. Feature requests prioritized by revenue impact. Competitive positioning sharp and current. The GTM function is not just selling; it is shaping what gets built next.
