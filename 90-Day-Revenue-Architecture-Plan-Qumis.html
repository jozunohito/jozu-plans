The Plan

90 Days to a Working Sales Engine

JEFF
ARAGON
Phase 1: Days 1-30
Audit, Target, Launch Outbound
✓ Audit the existing pipeline. Map every inbound deal, pilot, and conversation to date. Understand what triggered each one, what the buying process looked like, and what made 100% of pilots convert. Extract the patterns that will drive outbound targeting.
✓ Define the enterprise sales process. Document the ideal buyer journey from first touch to signed contract. Identify decision-makers at each stage: coverage counsel, claims VP, broker practice leader, procurement. Build qualification criteria specific to commercial P&C.
✓ Build the outbound target list. Remaining top 15 brokers, top 25 specialty carriers, and coverage law firms with 50+ attorneys. Research each account: who handles coverage analysis today, what their pain looks like, and who the economic buyer is.
✓ Develop ROI messaging. Quantify the value in terms buyers care about: coverage letter turnaround time reduced from days to minutes, outside counsel cost reduction, claims accuracy improvement. Build messaging that speaks their language, not SaaS language.
Phase 2: Days 31-60
Enterprise Deals, Repeatable Motion
✓ Launch outbound to top 50 targets. Execute multi-channel outreach to the highest-value accounts: personalized email sequences, LinkedIn engagement, direct calls to claims and coverage leadership. Lead with the pain of manual policy analysis, not the technology.
✓ Work live deals through procurement. Broker and carrier procurement is formal and slow. Navigate vendor onboarding, security reviews, compliance requirements. Build the internal materials and processes that make enterprise procurement repeatable.
✓ Close first outbound enterprise deals. Convert the strongest outbound opportunities into signed contracts. Prove the outbound model works alongside the inbound engine that already exists.
✓ Build the demo-to-close playbook. Document every step: discovery questions, demo flow, objection handling, proposal structure, procurement navigation. Make it repeatable so the next hire can follow it from day one.
Phase 3: Days 61-90
Scale Pipeline, Build the Team
✓ Scale the pipeline. Expand outbound across all three segments: brokers, carriers, and law firms. Pipeline predictable and growing. Outbound revenue supplementing the inbound engine with data to prove both channels.
✓ Hire the first AEs. Based on 60 days of real selling data, define the ideal AE profile. Write the job description, build the interview process, and start hiring. The playbook is ready for them to follow.
✓ Build the partnership channel. Insurance wholesalers, broker networks, and legal tech consultants are natural distribution partners. Identify the top 10 channel partners, structure pilot agreements, and launch the first partnerships.
✓ Present the 12-month revenue plan. Grounded in 90 days of real market data: conversion rates, deal sizes, cycle lengths, channel performance. A roadmap for scaling revenue through 2027 with clear hiring, pipeline, and partnership milestones.
