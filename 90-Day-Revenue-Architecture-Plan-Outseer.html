The Plan

90 Days to Americas Revenue Growth

Phase 1: Days 1-30
Audit, Align, Accelerate
✓ Audit Americas pipeline and team. Complete assessment of existing pipeline quality, stage accuracy, and velocity. Evaluate current team capabilities, territory coverage, and quota attainment trends. Identify what is real, what is stalled, and what needs to be rebuilt.
✓ Analyze win/loss data. Review the last 12 months of closed-won and closed-lost deals. Where is Outseer winning against BioCatch and NICE Actimize? Where is it losing? Map the patterns to inform competitive positioning and deal strategy.
✓ Define account tiering. Segment the Americas book into tiers based on revenue potential, competitive exposure, and Nacha compliance urgency. Prioritize the accounts that can move fastest and deliver the most revenue impact.
✓ Align with CRO on targets. Build a working cadence with Shane Cumming. Agree on Americas revenue targets, pipeline coverage ratios, forecasting methodology, and reporting structure. Ensure full alignment before scaling execution.
Phase 2: Days 31-60
Activate Demand, Build Competitive Edge
✓ Activate the Nacha compliance mandate. The March 2026 rule requiring proactive fraud monitoring is a demand catalyst. Build targeted outreach and deal strategy around compliance urgency. Every bank that has not implemented proactive monitoring is a prospect with a deadline.
✓ Accelerate enterprise pipeline. Focus on large FI opportunities where Outseer's data network and scale create a defensible advantage. Drive multi-product deals combining Fraud Manager, 3-D Secure, and FraudAction for maximum deal size and stickiness.
✓ Build competitive positioning against BioCatch. BioCatch leads in analyst perception. Outseer leads in transaction volume, data breadth, and installed base. Build battle cards, proof points, and competitive displacement playbooks that shift the conversation to where Outseer wins.
✓ Drive cross-sell across the installed base. 6,000+ FIs already use Outseer products. Many use only one. Identify cross-sell opportunities for Fraud Manager, 3-D Secure, and FraudAction within existing accounts. Fastest path to revenue growth.
Phase 3: Days 61-90
Scale the Engine
✓ Deliver Americas revenue plan. Present a data-backed plan for the next 4 quarters. Pipeline targets by segment, territory assignments, conversion benchmarks, and revenue forecast. Built on 60 days of real pipeline data, not assumptions.
✓ Establish partnership channel. Identify and activate partnerships with payment processors, banking technology consultants, and compliance advisory firms. These partners are already in conversations with the buyers Outseer needs to reach.
✓ Define team expansion needs. Based on pipeline analysis and territory coverage gaps, recommend hiring plan for the Americas sales organization. Roles, profiles, territories, and comp structures. Built to support the growth trajectory STG expects.
