Channel Partners

Building Indirect Revenue Streams

Direct enterprise sales will drive the majority of near-term revenue. But a strategic partnership channel accelerates pipeline, extends market reach, and creates trust signals that shorten deal cycles. These partnerships should be activated in Phase 2 and formalized by the end of Phase 3.

ERP and Financial System Integrators

SAP, Oracle, and Workday consulting firms are already embedded inside the finance organizations Trustmi needs to reach. Partners like Deloitte (already a Trustmi customer), Accenture, and KPMG can position Trustmi within active ERP transformation projects where payment security is a natural conversation.

Cybersecurity Channel Partners

MSSPs and cybersecurity VARs serve as trusted advisors to CISOs. Trustmi fills a gap in most security portfolios: payment fraud prevention that goes beyond email filtering. Partners like Optiv, WWT, and GuidePoint Security can bundle Trustmi into broader enterprise security assessments.

Payment and Treasury Platforms

Treasury management systems, accounts payable automation vendors, and payment processors are adjacent platforms. Integration partnerships with companies in the Tipalti, Coupa, or Kyriba ecosystem create embedded distribution where Trustmi enhances the platform's value proposition with fraud prevention.

Compliance and Risk Advisory Firms

As payment fraud regulations tighten, compliance consultants are advising clients on vendor risk management and payment controls. Trustmi's Vendor Onboarding module and Payment Flows product align directly with compliance advisory engagements, creating warm introductions to qualified buyers.
