# Fastino Unsolicited Email to Founders

Send after LinkedIn connect goes 48 hours without response, or in parallel with a different founder. Subject: a working subject line, three variants below. Body uses the new M/O: applied today, plan built, decline to share pre-discovery, request 20-minute call, opens with one discovery question.

---

## Subject line options

1. `Founding Head of Sales application + one question for you`
2. `Applied today for Founding Head of Sales (Ashby) + a question`
3. `Founding sales at Fastino: applied + one question`

Recommend option 1 for the default founder. Option 3 is the cleanest if there is concern about subject-line fatigue.

---

## Email body

```
Hi [FOUNDER FIRST NAME],

I applied through Ashby today for the Founding Head of Sales role. I wanted to reach out directly because this role fits the shape of work I did at Series A Ushur: AI workflow automation, individual contributor lead, founding-sales motion alongside the founders, Fortune 500 and regulated enterprise buyers. $8M ARR and $24M TCV in under two years on that motion.

One question before anything else: when a CFO at one of your design-partner enterprises asks for a per-workflow inference cost report on their existing frontier-API spend, what is the typical economic gap they find against running that same workflow on a Fastino specialized model?

Asking because that gap, named in writing, is the Power Message I would build the founding outbound voice around. It is also the gap I would expect to see drive the Series A revenue story.

I built a 90-day plan for this seat. McKinsey-style, structured around three load-bearing initiatives, with workflow wedge library and Microsoft Azure Marketplace co-sell paths included. I am not going to send it before we talk. Pre-discovery shipping a plan is the wrong order of operations and not how I would run a Fastino enterprise deal either. The plan is the artifact of the conversation, not the opener for it.

What I am asking for is 20 minutes. I will use the time to validate the wedge against your real customer data, share the parallel pattern from Ushur, and let you decide whether I belong in the Founding Head of Sales process.

I am in Coeur d'Alene, Idaho, remote-first and travel-ready for Bay Area cadence. Calendly link or any 20-minute slot you propose works.

Thanks for the consideration,

Jeff Aragon
Founder, Jozu Consulting Group
linkedin.com/in/jeffreyaragon
jeff@jozuconsultinggroup.com
```

---

## Notes for sending

- Personalize `[FOUNDER FIRST NAME]` with the verified founder name from Fastino's website or LinkedIn. Do not use a name from training data.
- AI-assisted opener is implicit here through the discovery question and structure. The voice is direct and operator-grade. No em dashes. No mid-sentence bolding.
- The discovery question is the only question in the email. It is calibrated to a real economic gap (per-workflow inference cost) that Fastino's product wedge addresses. It signals operator-grade discovery muscle without being a generic "what keeps you up at night" question.
- The plan is referenced as an asset, not shared. This matches the "plan is a reserve asset, not a lead asset" rule.
- Close with a direct ask (20 minutes) and a posture answer (location, remote-ready, travel-ready). No hedging.
