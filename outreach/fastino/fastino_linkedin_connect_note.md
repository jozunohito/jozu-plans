Use one of the variants below. Each is under 300 characters. Personalize `[FOUNDER FIRST NAME]` with the actual founder's first name from LinkedIn before sending.

Note on names: TechCrunch coverage of Fastino's $25M seed identifies the founding team out of Google Research, Apple, Stanford, and Cambridge. Confirm the current founder roster on Fastino's website (fastino.ai) and LinkedIn before sending. Do not use a name from training data without verification.

## Default recommendation

Send Variant A. It anchors directly on Jeff's strongest credential (Ushur AI workflow automation, IC, founder-attached, regulated buyers) and matches the JD shape word for word. Variants B and C are alternates if Variant A has already been used with a different founder on the same team.

## Sequencing

1. Send LinkedIn connect with Variant A to the most-active founder on LinkedIn first.
2. Wait 48 hours.
3. If accepted with no reply, send the unsolicited email follow-up (`fastino_unsolicited_email_to_founders.md`).
4. If no acceptance, send Variant B to the second founder.
5. Track each touch in the outreach log.
