The Plan

90 Days to a Working Sales Engine

JEFF
ARAGON
Phase 1: Days 1-30
Foundation
✓ Buyer definition. Define the marketing buyer persona with precision - VP Marketing, Head of Demand Gen, or CMO at what company size and stage. Identify which marketing functions (demand gen, content ops, campaign management) drive the purchase decision and which use cases create the most urgency.
✓ Power Message. Build a message that goes beyond AI marketing automation. Connect JustAI's agentic capabilities to specific marketing outcomes - pipeline attribution, campaign ROI, and the operational cost of marketing workflows that currently require 3-4 point solutions.
✓ Discovery framework. Create a discovery framework that uncovers the prospect's current marketing stack, what is working, what is not, and where manual effort creates bottlenecks. Focus on quantifying the cost of their current approach so the value of switching becomes obvious.
✓ Pipeline architecture. Design a pipeline for marketing tech sales. Marketing buyers evaluate tools through demos, trials, and team feedback. Build stages that track these buyer actions and flag deals that stall in evaluation without progressing to team adoption.
Phase 2: Days 31-60
Execution
✓ Outbound activation. Launch outbound targeting VP Marketing and Demand Gen leaders at companies with 50-500 employees. Sequences built around the specific pain of fragmented marketing stacks and the promise of consolidated, AI-driven execution.
✓ Live deal work. Work live deals with the founding team. Focus on demonstrating measurable impact during the evaluation period. Build a 2-week proof-of-value framework that shows JustAI delivering results on a real marketing campaign, not just a product demo.
✓ Sequence of Events. Map the marketing tech buying journey from first demo to signed contract. Include the trial period, team feedback loop, stack integration review, and budget approval. Marketing buyers want consensus from their team before committing.
✓ Forecast discipline. Implement pipeline reviews with martech-specific qualification criteria. Track trial engagement, team adoption signals, and competitive evaluation status. Marketing tool deals die when the trial does not show clear results - build early warning triggers.
Phase 3: Days 61-90
Scale Readiness
✓ Closed revenue. Close 3-5 early customers with documented marketing impact metrics. These accounts become proof points that differentiate JustAI from every other AI marketing tool making unsubstantiated claims.
✓ Repeatable process. Package the complete martech sales playbook - buyer personas, competitive positioning, discovery framework, proof-of-value methodology, and deal progression criteria. This is the system your Founding AE runs from day one.
✓ Hiring blueprint. Define the Founding AE profile for martech sales. This person needs to understand marketing operations, speak credibly to marketing leaders, and sell against a crowded field of AI tools. Write the scorecard and interview process to find that person.
✓ Handoff. Transfer the complete sales system, active pipeline, and proof-of-value materials to your Founding AE. Provide 30 days of deal coaching to ensure they can replicate results independently.
