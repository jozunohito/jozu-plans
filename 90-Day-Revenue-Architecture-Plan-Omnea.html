The Plan

90 Days to a US Revenue Engine

JEFF
ARAGON
Phase 1: Days 1-30
Learn the Product, Map the US Market
✓ Deep product immersion. Learn the platform end to end. Understand how Omnea sits on top of Coupa, SAP Ariba, and other P2P systems. Know every integration, every workflow, every competitive differentiation point cold.
✓ Define the US ICP. Work with Ben, Arie, and the UK sales team to translate the existing customer profile into a US enterprise target list. Map industries, company sizes, and procurement maturity levels where Omnea wins fastest. Build the first 200-account target list.
✓ Develop CFO and CPO messaging. Procurement leaders in US enterprises think differently than UK buyers. Build messaging frameworks that lead with intake chaos, approval bottlenecks, and P2P fragmentation - the problems that keep CFOs and CPOs up at night.
✓ Study existing wins. Dissect how Spotify, MongoDB, and Albertsons were sold and onboarded. Identify patterns in deal cycle, stakeholder map, objections, and time to value. These stories become the foundation for every US conversation.
Phase 2: Days 31-60
Launch Outbound, Close First US Deals
✓ Launch enterprise outbound. Execute multi-channel campaigns targeting CFOs, CPOs, and VPs of Procurement at target accounts. Email sequences, LinkedIn, warm introductions, and strategic cold outreach. Lead with the overlay story - no rip-and-replace.
✓ Navigate multi-stakeholder deals. Enterprise procurement purchases involve procurement, finance, IT, and compliance. Run discovery across all stakeholders, build internal champions, and manage the buying committee through evaluation to close.
✓ Close first US enterprise deals. Push qualified opportunities through the full cycle. Build the ROI case for each buyer: current cost of manual intake and approval workflows vs. Omnea automation. Make the business case undeniable.
✓ Run C-suite events. Organize intimate executive roundtables and dinners in New York targeting procurement and finance leaders. Build the peer network that accelerates enterprise trust and shortens deal cycles.
Phase 3: Days 61-90
Scale Pipeline, Build the Playbook
✓ Scale US pipeline. Multiple enterprise opportunities in play across verticals. Pipeline growing predictably through outbound, events, and referrals from early customers. Revenue trajectory validated with real data.
✓ Build the playbook for next hires. Document the entire US GTM motion: ICP criteria, outbound sequences, discovery frameworks, demo flows, competitive positioning against Zip, objection handling, and close process. Everything the next AE needs to ramp fast.
✓ Define vertical focus. Based on 90 days of real selling, identify which US verticals convert fastest and have the highest ACV. Financial services, healthcare, technology, retail - the data will show where to concentrate.
✓ Present the US revenue plan. Deliver a data-backed plan to Ben and Arie covering pipeline projections, hiring needs, territory strategy, and the path to the 10x ARR target. Not a slide deck - a working model built from real pipeline data.
