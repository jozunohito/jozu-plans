# SOURCING UNIVERSE DOCTRINE

**Version:** v1.0, 2026-04-22
**Scope:** every commercially viable company on earth that fits one of two tiers below.

## Tier rubric (first principles)

**Tier 1 — Jeff Aragon direct role targets.**
Trigger: company has an open, publicly verifiable job posting for any of the following senior GTM roles.
- Chief Revenue Officer (CRO), Chief Sales Officer, Chief Commercial Officer.
- Head of Sales, VP Sales, VP Worldwide Sales.
- Head of GTM, VP GTM, Head of Revenue.
- Founding Sales, Founding AE, Founding GTM Leader, Founding Revenue Hire.
- Fractional CRO, Fractional Head of Sales.
Action: direct Jeff outreach and tailored application. Built Plan Letter if the role warrants it.

**Tier 2 — Jozu Consulting target prospects.**
Trigger: company shows one or more signals of commercial viability and near-term GTM need.
Signals:
- Funded (venture-backed minimum; priority Series A through B+ with a round in the last 18 months).
- Launching or scaling US sales (US expansion announcements, US office opening, first US commercial hire, funding earmarked for US GTM).
- Japan HQ with US ambitions (highest-fit segment given Jeff's specialty).
- International HQ (Israel, Europe, APAC, LATAM) with US GTM gap.
- Founder-led sales motion that has hit product-market fit and is ready to scale.
- Post-acquisition integration creating GTM rebuild needs.
- Accelerator graduation moving toward scale phase.
- Revenue stuck at ceiling that correlates with sales-process gap (e.g., reps capped at $75K-$200K deal sizes for a product that should carry $1M+).

Action: signal classification then split into two substreams:
- **Tier 2a (Jeff's manual outreach):** strategically important, highest-signal, warrants Jeff's personal built Plan Letter + manual LinkedIn + tailored email. Excluded from RGI automation.
- **Tier 2b (RGI + AI-powered automation):** commercially viable Jozu prospect, bulk outreach via RGI BDR team and AI-powered LinkedIn avatar accounts ($400/mo for 2 avatars), with templated Plan Letters.

Engagement fit by Tier 2 company stage:
- Pre-seed / seed: GTM Foundation Sprint ($2,500/mo, 3 months).
- Series A: System Install or System + Operator ($2,500-$5,000/mo, 3 months).
- Series B+: System + Operator or Fractional CRO ($5,000-$20,000/mo, 90 days to 12 months).

## Classification rubric (for each surfaced company)

Per candidate, capture:
1. Company name.
2. HQ country / city.
3. Stage (pre-seed, seed, Series A, B, C, etc.).
4. Last funding (amount + date).
5. Lead investor / investor names.
6. Vertical (B2B SaaS, AI/ML, fintech, healthtech, dev tools, cybersecurity, enterprise, industry-specific).
7. Open role (if any): title + seniority + URL.
8. US launch signal (specific evidence if any).
9. Japan fit flag (Y/N).
10. Tier classification: 1, 2a, 2b.
11. Fit score (1-10) on Jeff's CRO profile or Jozu engagement fit.
12. Source of signal (YC directory, VC portfolio, LinkedIn post, Crunchbase article, etc.).
13. Date found.
