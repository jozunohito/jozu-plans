## The rule — VARIANT-SPECIFIC, OPPOSITE BY VARIANT

**This rule depends on which resume variant is being used. Get it wrong and the application is dead on arrival.**

### VP / Head-of-Sales / RVP-tier variant (sub-CRO seats)

- **Lead Jozu's identity with Japan-to-US market entry.** That is the primary practice on this variant.
- **DO NOT mention "Fractional CRO" anywhere.** Not in the narrative paragraph. Not in any bullet. Not in the summary section. Anywhere it appears = a flight-risk signal that kills the application. Hiring manager reads: "He's running a parallel revenue practice. He'll bail when his consulting picks up." This killed runZero. It will kill others.
- Japan-to-US framing is the OPPOSITE of flight-risk-flavored — it signals rare specialty (very few US enterprise sellers can bridge Japan), narrow practice (not a generalist consulting shop), and unique value-add to the employer.

### CRO master variant (CRO and equivalent C-level revenue seats)

- **MUST include "Fractional CRO" as a primary service line.** Jeff is applying to CRO seats. Jozu's CRO advisory practice is the CREDIBILITY WEDGE, not a flight risk. Critical.
- A candidate applying to CRO who runs a Fractional CRO practice signals operator-grade CRO capability — has done the work for multiple companies, not just one seat-holder's perspective. This is a strength.
- Japan-to-US should ALSO be present as a second practice line — it's a real specialty and a differentiator at the CRO level. But Fractional CRO is the lead.
- Vanta-style variants (CRO-tier targeted) follow this same rule.

### Why these are opposite

| | VP variant | CRO variant |
|---|---|---|
| Fractional CRO on resume | **Forbidden** | **Required** |
| Reason | Flight risk for sub-CRO seat | Credibility wedge for CRO seat |
| Japan-to-US | Lead identity | Secondary practice line |
| Hiring-manager read of "I run a Fractional CRO practice" | "He'll bail" | "He's done this for multiple companies" |

**If in doubt about which variant to use:** match to the title of the role being applied to. CRO / Chief Revenue Officer / Chief Sales Officer = CRO variant. VP Sales / Regional VP / Head of Sales / Head of Revenue at sub-CRO scope = VP variant.

## How to apply — Jozu Consulting Group resume section templates

### VP variant template (sub-CRO seats)

```
### Jozu Consulting Group | Founder
[Year] to Present | [City, State]

Founded Jozu Consulting Group as a Japan-to-US market entry practice, helping Japanese technology companies build go-to-market infrastructure and revenue traction in the United States. The Jozu Enterprise Revenue System, installed inside client engagements, took shape during 5.5 years at Kronos (now UKG), where precision execution of Power Messaging and Plan Letter discipline was the operational standard, and was refined through concurrent Sales Process Coaching at CustomerCentric Selling since 2014.

- Design and execute US market-entry GTM for Japanese technology companies, including ICP definition, Power Messaging, discovery architecture, Plan Letter discipline, and mutual action plans tuned for US enterprise buyers.
- Adapt messaging and value framing across the cultural and operational gaps between Japanese product teams and US enterprise procurement.
- Operate as embedded sales leader inside multi-stakeholder bridge environments where decisions require alignment across Japanese executive teams (nemawashi and ringi), US partners, and end-buyer procurement committees.
```

DO NOT add a "Fractional CRO" bullet. DO NOT reduce Japan-to-US to a single line. DO NOT frame Jozu as generic "advisory" without the Japan-to-US specificity.

### CRO master variant template (CRO and C-level revenue seats)

```
### Jozu Consulting Group | Founder
[Year] to Present | [City, State]

Founded Jozu Consulting Group to deliver Fractional CRO engagements and Japan-to-US market entry advisory, installing the Jozu Enterprise Revenue System inside early-stage and growth-stage technology companies. The methodology took shape during 5.5 years at Kronos (now UKG), where precision execution of Power Messaging and Plan Letter discipline was the operational standard, and was refined through concurrent Sales Process Coaching at CustomerCentric Selling since 2014.

- Deliver Fractional CRO engagements and full-cycle enterprise deal execution for early-stage and growth-stage technology companies, installing permanent revenue infrastructure including ICP definition, Power Messaging, discovery architecture, Plan Letter discipline, and mutual action plans.
- Design and execute US market-entry GTM for Japanese technology companies bringing AI, automation, and enterprise SaaS products to American enterprise buyers.
- Operate as embedded sales leader inside multi-stakeholder environments where decisions require alignment across executive, operational, and procurement stakeholders, including Japan-side nemawashi and ringi alignment when relevant.
```

DO NOT remove "Fractional CRO" from the lead. DO NOT reduce Fractional CRO to a side note. The Fractional CRO practice is the credibility wedge for the CRO seat.

## What about cover letters and outreach?

Same rule. When mentioning Jozu in a cover note or LinkedIn message:
- Lead: "I run Jozu Consulting Group, a Japan-to-US market entry practice." OR similar.
- Never: "I run a fractional CRO practice."
- Never: "I deliver fractional engagements."

If the application is for a CRO seat at a Japan-bridge-relevant company, the Japan-to-US framing is itself the wedge. If the application is for a non-Japan role, the Japan-to-US framing still positions Jozu as a niche bridge practice, not a generalist consulting shop competing with the seat.
