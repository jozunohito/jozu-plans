The Plan

90 Days to a Working Sales Engine

JEFF
ARAGON
Phase 1: Days 1-30
Audit, Focus, and Build the Foundation
✓ Audit the existing sales motion. Map every current deal, lead source, and conversion path. Understand what is working organically and where deals stall. Identify the fastest path to repeatable revenue.
✓ Define ICP and vertical focus. Analyze which brand categories and industries convert best and retain longest. Prioritize verticals where BrandBastion's AI-plus-human model creates the clearest differentiation against Sprinklr, Khoros, and Brandwatch.
✓ Build the enterprise target list. Research and prioritize 200+ enterprise brands by vertical, social media spend, and brand safety exposure. Build the prospecting infrastructure and outreach sequences.
✓ Hire first 2 AEs. Write the role profiles, source candidates, and begin interviews. Hire closers who can sell into brand marketing and social media leadership at enterprise scale.
Phase 2: Days 31-60
Launch Outbound, Build Pipeline Discipline
✓ Launch structured outbound. Execute multi-channel outbound campaigns targeting priority verticals. Lead with the HBS case study, the customer proof points, and the measurable ROI of AI-powered engagement management at scale.
✓ Build sales stages with clear exit criteria. Define what qualifies an opportunity to move forward at each stage. No deal advances without meeting specific criteria. This is how you build a forecast you can trust.
✓ Implement HubSpot discipline. Clean pipeline data, consistent stage management, activity tracking, and reporting. Every rep follows the same process. Every deal is visible.
✓ Close first outbound enterprise deals. Push qualified opportunities through the full cycle. Use the Netflix, Uber, and Sephora proof points to compress sales cycles and build buyer confidence.
Phase 3: Days 61-90
Scale Pipeline, Build Channels, Plan for Growth
✓ Scale pipeline across verticals. Expand outbound into secondary verticals based on Phase 1 and Phase 2 learnings. Pipeline growing predictably with clear conversion metrics by vertical and channel.
✓ Build partnership channel. Identify and engage social media agencies and brand consultancies that serve enterprise clients. These partners recommend tools to their clients daily. Build a channel that generates pipeline without adding headcount.
✓ Refine pricing and packaging. Based on 90 days of live selling, refine pricing tiers and packaging to maximize deal size and reduce friction. Align packaging to buyer segments and use cases.
✓ Present 12-month revenue plan. Deliver a data-backed revenue plan to Jenny with targets, headcount needs, channel strategy, and investment requirements. Built on real pipeline data, not projections from a spreadsheet.
