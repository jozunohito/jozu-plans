The Plan

90 Days to a Working Sales Engine

Phase 1: Days 1-30
Own the Pipeline, Close Deals
✓ Close inbound immediately. Field and close the existing inbound leads and demos. Learn the product, the buyer, and the sales cycle by closing deals on day one. Understand why people buy and where deals stall.
✓ Launch outbound. Hunt for ideal customers. Build the outbound motion targeting marketing leaders at companies investing in SEO and paid search who have not started optimizing for AI answers. Lead with the data: 7B visits, 76% growth, and the competitive gap.
✓ Sit with R&D. Share key pain points and sales objections the team should build against. The product feedback loop starts day one, not after a ramp period.
✓ Build the sales data foundation. Own the CRM. Clean pipeline. Accurate data. Build the source of truth for sales that supports scaling to 10x the team in the future.
Phase 2: Days 31-60
Scale the Motion, Throw Events
✓ Outbound producing pipeline. Sequences tested and iterating. Reply rates improving. Target accounts identified and being worked. The outbound motion is becoming a system, not ad hoc hustle.
✓ Events launched. Throw dedicated events for prospects and customers. Intimate dinners, roundtables, meetups. Position Gauge as the authority on AI search optimization. Every event generates 10+ qualified conversations.
✓ Expansion revenue. Work existing customers to expand usage. Strong retention and expansion is already happening. Build the systematic approach: identify expansion triggers, run QBRs, propose new use cases.
✓ Category messaging sharpened. Work with the CEO to refine positioning. AI search optimization is a new category. The messaging must educate and sell simultaneously. Test what resonates and document it.
Phase 3: Days 61-90
Revenue Engine Running
✓ Revenue accelerating. Multiple deals closing per month from both inbound and outbound. The 30%+ MoM growth rate is maintained or accelerated with a dedicated sales function.
✓ Systems built for scale. CRM, reporting, pipeline management, and sales processes all built to support 10x the team. The next hires plug into a working system.
✓ Playbook documented. The complete sales motion: ICP, outbound sequences, inbound conversion, demo framework, objection handling, pricing strategy, event playbook. Ready for the team to scale.
✓ Path from seven to nine figures mapped. Based on 90 days of real data: what the sales org needs to look like, which segments to expand into, which channels to invest in, what the hiring plan is. The strategy to go from seven figures to nine.
