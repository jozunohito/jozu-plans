Product Overview

The Above Platform

Above deploys a fleet of specialized AI investigators that continuously analyze behavior across identity systems, endpoints, SaaS platforms, and AI environments. The Arbiter engine correlates behavioral signals to determine intent, not just anomalies, producing investigation-ready timelines for security, legal, and HR teams.

Core Differentiators

Intent-Based Detection

Reconstructs activity narratives and determines intent rather than flagging anomalies. Dramatically reduces false positives compared to legacy UEBA and DLP tools. Produces evidentiary timelines the moment activity becomes an incident.

Agentic AI Coverage

Treats AI agents as insiders requiring the same level of behavioral oversight as human employees. Monitors OAuth connections, API access, and autonomous actions. Purpose-built for the expanding attack surface of agentic enterprise environments.

Zero-Config Deployment

Enterprise customers deploy within minutes without predefined rules, policies, or configuration. No extensive tuning period. No manual policy creation. Time-to-value measured in hours, not months.

Coaching-First Methodology

Guides employees away from risky behavior proactively rather than blocking access. Reduces friction with end users while preventing negligent incidents, which account for 45% of all breaches (IBM). Validated by enterprise CISOs.

Specialized AI Agent Fleet

Shadow AI & IT Agent

Monitors SaaS apps, OAuth connections, browser extensions, clipboard activity. Unifies fragmented behavioral signals across platforms.

Data Exfiltration Agent

Combines behavioral patterns, user permissions, and communication context to distinguish accidental from malicious data movement.

Flight Risk Agent

Tracks job-search signals, behavioral shifts, access pattern changes. Separates genuine flight risks from normal employee transitions.

Inappropriate Use Agent

Identifies misuse in real-time before escalation. Provides corrective guidance rather than blocking access.

Communications Agent

Sentiment analysis across collaboration platforms. Links tone and communication patterns to behavioral risk signals.

Arbiter Engine

Orchestrates all agents. Correlates signals like a human investigator. Produces full evidentiary timelines with intent determination.

Enterprise Integrations

Native integrations with CrowdStrike, Splunk, Microsoft Sentinel, Palo Alto XSOAR, and leading identity, endpoint, and SaaS platforms. Automated compliance reporting for NIST 800-171, CMMC, GDPR, HIPAA, and other regulatory frameworks.

Why Now: The Agentic Inflection Point

The insider threat market is undergoing a fundamental shift. As enterprises deploy AI agents that access internal systems and act autonomously on behalf of employees, the perimeter of "who counts as an insider" is expanding faster than any existing tool was built to handle. Legacy DLP and UEBA solutions were designed for a world where insiders were human and data movement was the primary risk signal.

Enterprise Buyer Pain

  • Alert fatigue from high false-positive rates in legacy tools
  • Investigation timelines measured in weeks, not hours
  • No visibility into AI agent behavior within enterprise environments
  • Hybrid/remote work has dissolved traditional perimeters
  • Regulatory pressure accelerating (CMMC, GDPR, HIPAA, SOX)
  • Board-level focus on data exfiltration and IP protection

Market Tailwinds

  • 45% of breaches caused by negligent insiders (IBM) - not malicious actors
  • Agentic AI adoption creating entirely new insider risk surface
  • Cloud and SaaS sprawl multiplying data access points
  • M&A activity increasing data exposure during transitions
  • BYOD and shadow IT policies creating blind spots
  • Insurance carriers beginning to require insider threat controls

Above Security's Market Position

Above enters a market dominated by legacy tools, at a moment when the problem is being redefined by AI agent proliferation. The combination of intent-based detection, zero-config deployment, and coaching-first methodology creates a platform that enterprise buyers have not seen before. Early customer validation (Merlin Entertainments deploying in minutes) confirms product-market fit with large enterprises.
