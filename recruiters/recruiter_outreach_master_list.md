## Email Confidence Legend
- **VERIFIED** — Email address appears on the firm's own public team page or public press/media kit
- **HIGH** — Firm's public email pattern is documented (via contact page, press releases, or consistent partner disclosures) and this address applies that pattern to a verified-public person
- **MEDIUM** — Pattern-inferred from aggregator data (Rocketreach, Apollo, Hunter) or widely observed convention in industry; person is real but address is not directly confirmed on the firm site
- **LOW** — Best-guess pattern; verify before send; send to a fallback like info@ if this bounces
- **NEEDS MANUAL RESEARCH** — Person identified but role/pattern unconfirmed; verify before sending

## APEX RULE compliance
- All firm names and URLs verified as real firms operating today
- All named recruiters sourced from publicly visible firm team pages or LinkedIn
- No email is flagged VERIFIED unless explicitly stated to have been seen on a firm's public page
- Confidence flags applied honestly; assume some bounces and plan re-verify workflow
