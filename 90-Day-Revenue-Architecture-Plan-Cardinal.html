The Plan

90 Days to a Working Sales Engine

ARAGON
Phase 1: Days 1-30
Run Deals, Use the Product, Win
✓ Sell from day one. Run full-cycle sales from first touch to close. Take existing pipeline, generate new opportunities, and close deals. Learn the product by selling it and by using it to prospect.
✓ Use Cardinal to sell Cardinal. Run personal outbound through the platform. Experience the product as a user. Understand what works, what is hard, and what delights. Every customer conversation is better when you have used the tool yourself.
✓ Get customers to first response fast. Work closely with customers to launch and optimize their outbound campaigns. The faster they see results, the faster they expand. Time-to-first-response is the metric that matters.
✓ Creative outbound strategies. Develop campaigns that are not templates. Use Cardinal's signal detection, content monitoring, and personalization to build outbound that feels human because it is grounded in real shared context.
Phase 2: Days 31-60
Repeatable Systems, Account Expansion
✓ Turn wins into systems. Take successful campaigns and turn them into repeatable playbooks. Document what works: which signals matter, which personalization approaches convert, which sequences drive replies. Make it teachable.
✓ Expand accounts. Current customers using Cardinal for one use case should be using it for five. Identify expansion opportunities: new teams, new channels, new campaign types. Drive upsell revenue.
✓ Product feedback loop. Push product forward with real customer feedback. What features close deals? What gaps lose them? What do customers ask for most? Bridge the gap between customers and the product team.
✓ Close high-value deals. Move beyond the YC network. Target sophisticated sales teams at larger companies who are stringing together 10+ tools for outbound. Position Cardinal as the replacement for the entire stack.
Phase 3: Days 61-90
Scale Revenue, Define the Category
✓ Revenue accelerating. Multiple deals closing per month. Pipeline growing. ARR trajectory justifies the next phase of team growth.
✓ Playbook documented. The complete sales motion documented: discovery framework, demo structure, campaign launch process, objection handling, competitive positioning, pricing strategy. Ready for the next hire.
✓ Category definition. Cardinal is not just another outbound tool. It is the AI platform for precision outbound. The messaging, the demos, the customer stories all reinforce this positioning. Help define how modern outbound works.
✓ Beyond YC. Customer base expanding beyond the YC ecosystem. Enterprise and mid-market companies adopting Cardinal. The product is proven with sophisticated early adopters and now scaling to the broader market.
