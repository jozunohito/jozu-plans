The Plan

90 Days to a Working Sales Engine

JEFF
ARAGON
Phase 1: Days 1-30
Close Deals, Learn the Agent
✓ Sell from day one. Take ownership of existing pipeline and new inbound. Prospect, qualify, run demos, and push deals toward close. Learn the product by selling it, not by reading docs. Understand what objections come up, what builds trust, and what makes a buyer say yes to autonomous agents in their workflows.
✓ Learn to build agents on the platform. Get hands-on with the Asteroid platform. Scope customer workflows end to end. Assess implementation complexity. The credibility to sell this product comes from being able to sit with engineering and articulate exactly what the agent will do and how.
✓ Map the healthcare buyer. Asteroid was first in healthcare. Define the buying process: Heads of Operations, Practice Managers, Clinicians, IT leaders. Who has budget authority? What triggers an evaluation? What compliance and security requirements gate the deal?
✓ Product feedback from the front lines. Aggregate every customer conversation into actionable product insights. What features close deals? What gaps lose them? What do buyers wish the agents could do? Feed this directly to engineering.
Phase 2: Days 31-60
Build the Outbound Engine and Close Six Figures
✓ Launch outbound. Build and run the outbound engine end to end. ICP experiments across healthcare operations, insurance, and other verticals with mission-critical workflows. Test messaging, channels, and sequences. Find the repeatable customer pockets.
✓ Close a six-figure deal. Navigate the full enterprise cycle: technical evaluation, operational scoping, security and compliance review, economic buyer alignment, negotiation, close. Prove the six-figure motion works.
✓ Content and inbound strategy. Own the content strategy for LinkedIn, X, and SEO/GEO. Publish insights from real customer deployments. Position Asteroid as the authority on computer-use agents for mission-critical operations. Inbound is a compound investment. Start now.
✓ Account management. Be the commercial face post-sale. Manage relationships, support agent launches, stay close to customers as they expand. Happy customers become references, which is the most powerful sales tool in a trust sale.
Phase 3: Days 61-90
Repeatable Motion, Defensible Position
✓ Pipeline is predictable. Outbound generating consistent qualified meetings. Inbound starting to compound. CRM is clean, pipeline is visible, forecast is data-driven. The commercial function runs.
✓ PMF iteration. Based on 90 days of live selling, refine the ICP, positioning, and messaging. PMF is a moving target at every stage. Document what changed and why so the next iteration is faster.
✓ Expansion revenue from existing accounts. Current customers expanding usage. Account expansion playbook documented: new workflows, new departments, increased volume. Land and expand is working.
✓ Pricing and packaging validated. Pricing tested against real deals. Packaging optimized for conversion and expansion. Compliance and operational pricing considerations documented. Ready to scale.
