## SLIDE 3 — Why I Believe Snowball Wins the Brand-Content Category

**Header:**
> Why I Believe Snowball Wins the Category

**Three bullets, equal weight:**

**1. Inbound at scale is a qualification problem, not a sales problem.**
Public signals point to ~90% inbound at Snowball. That isn't solved by adding sellers. It's solved by a qualification rubric, a reframe, and a Plan Letter cycle that converts the right inbound and politely declines the rest. That discipline is mine.

**2. 12–18 month build-and-exit engagements need a designed second motion.**
You either renew, expand into adjacent surfaces, or leave behind a net-zero-effort referral asset. All three are solvable. Which one Snowball wants is the conversation.

**3. "Great products, garbage channels" is the buyer I have closed for 25 years.**
Healthcare buyers are the same psychological customer as the brands you serve. Same procurement, same multi-stakeholder buying committee, different surface. $24M TCV, $10M largest deal, $150M career revenue closed in that exact buyer archetype.
