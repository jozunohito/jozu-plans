## Voice rules honored across all 11 kits

- No em dashes
- "Will" not "would" wherever defensible
- "Hello" not "Hey"
- "Thank you" sign-off
- Humble assassin (operating numbers, not comparative diminishment)
- No personal earnings / commissions figures
- Plan-as-reserve-asset (mentioned, never linked or attached as URL)
- Application date referenced in every direct email
- Paste-ready contiguous-paragraph format inside fenced code blocks
- Canonical methodology paragraph used in every direct email

## Stop conditions (apply to all 11)

- Reply from decision-maker → suspend cadence, schedule call, walk 90-day plan verbally
- Reply from peer or investor → engage that thread, suspend cold cadence
- Decline → log in `applications_log.md`, thank-you reply, end cadence
- Active recruiting screen → enter screen, suspend cold cadence
