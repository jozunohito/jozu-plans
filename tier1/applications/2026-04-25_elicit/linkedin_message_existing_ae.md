## What this message does

- Opens with peer-to-peer framing (not over-the-head)
- Anchors the application date so the AE knows it ties to a real open req
- One paragraph of fit, anchored on CalOptima as the regulated-buyer analog
- Asks for 15 minutes to LEARN, not to pitch
- Soft warm-intro path: "if you think it useful, I would welcome the introduction" (humble, not pushy)
- Explicit graceful exit: "if not, I will have learned and appreciate the time either way"
- Plan-as-reserve-asset rule honored (no plan URL, no PDF)
- "Thank you" sign-off; "Jeff" alone is fine for a peer-register touch

## Tone check

- Humble assassin posture maintained
- No comparative diminishment
- No personal earnings figures
- "Will" not "would" wherever defensible
- Zero em dashes
- Not too folksy, not too stiff
