# PE Portfolio CRO / VP Sales / Head of Revenue Hunt
**Date:** 2026-04-24
**Method:** Direct fetches against PE portfolio job boards (Insight Partners, TA Associates, Francisco Partners, Vista, Permira, Serent, Silver Lake) + LinkedIn role searches + verification of PE backing per company
**Filters:** PE-backed software/SaaS, CRO / VP Sales / Head of Sales / Head of Revenue / SVP Sales titles, US-based or remote-friendly preferred

## Provenance Discipline

- "Verified live posting" = role title pulled directly from the PE firm's portfolio job board or LinkedIn job listing on 2026-04-24.
- "PE backing verified" = ownership confirmed via PE firm portfolio page, press release, or PitchBook/Crunchbase reference.
- Roles flagged "VC-backed not PE" where research showed venture funding rather than PE buyout — included for completeness because Jeff's filter notes flexibility on stage, but tagged honestly.
- Fit 1-5 reflects: ARR fit (under $100M ceiling preference), player-coach shape, vertical alignment to Jeff's track record (healthcare, fintech, RevOps platforms), remote posture.
- **No fabrication.** Where a role was searched but not found, that is stated as such. Where a portfolio job board returned only the default page (not keyword-filtered server-side), that limitation is logged.
