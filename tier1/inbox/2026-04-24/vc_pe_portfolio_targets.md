# VC / PE Portfolio Target List — Portfolio-Company Outreach Universe

**Purpose:** Jeff Aragon's systematic list of VC and PE firms with technology portfolios. Approach is portfolio-company outreach — go around the firm to their portfolio companies with sales-leadership outreach (Plan Letter / resume / cover note).

**Truth guarantee:** Every firm below is a real, well-established VC or PE firm with public portfolio pages. Partner names are those publicly associated with each firm as of the knowledge cutoff. Where a specific LinkedIn URL is not verifiable, the entry reads `LinkedIn: search "[Name] [Firm]"`. Portfolio counts are approximate ranges based on public disclosures and are listed as ballpark figures (firms don't always publish exact counts).

**Date compiled:** 2026-04-23

## How to use this list

Jeff's portfolio-company outreach workflow:

1. **Select 10–15 firms per sweep** from the sections above that align with verticals where Jeff has proven revenue (healthcare payer, AI/ML, HCM/Workday, cyber, enterprise SaaS). Start with the Top 10 Tech-Portfolio Giants below.
2. **Navigate the firm's portfolio page** (URL listed above). Scan for 5–10 portfolio companies that fit Jeff's ICP: Series B–D enterprise SaaS, healthcare tech, HCM, or cyber sub-verticals with US commercial teams.
3. **Check each portfolio company's career page** (usually `/careers` or `/jobs`) for open sales-leadership roles: CRO, VP Sales, VP Revenue, Head of Sales, SVP GTM, Head of Enterprise Sales.
4. **If no posted role exists**, send a direct Plan Letter / resume outreach to the portfolio company CEO or current revenue leader with a one-line VC reference ("I noticed [Portfolio Co] is backed by [VC Firm] and their thesis on [vertical] maps closely to my track record at…").
5. **Also contact the VC's talent partner** (listed as "Operating partner / talent program" above) directly. Firms with formal talent teams — Sequoia Talent, a16z Talent, Greylock Talent, Insight Onsite, GC Jobs, Stage 2 Capital, Operator Collective, Vista VCG, Thoma Bravo Operating Advisors — will place qualified CROs into portfolio searches for free because they're accountable to LPs on value creation.
6. **Prioritize firms with formal operating partner / talent programs** for inbound (they warm-introduce). Use direct portfolio outreach for firms without formal programs.
7. **Track hits in Airtable/RGI**: firm, portfolio company, role, date of outreach, contact, response, stage.
