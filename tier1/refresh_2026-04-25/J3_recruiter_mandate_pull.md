## TRUTH PREAMBLE — Read this first

Most retained-search firms (Korn Ferry, Egon Zehnder, Russell Reynolds, Heidrick, Spencer Stuart, ZRG, Daversa, True Search, Bespoke, SPMB, Hanold, ON Partners, Cowen, Riviera, Kindred, JM Search, Caldwell, Boyden, Stanton Chase, Kingsley Gate, DHR) **do NOT publish their active mandates publicly**. Their clients pay for confidentiality. Their websites show case studies / placements completed, not open seats.

What that means for J3:
- A small subset (Daversa, Bowdoin, Bespoke, Hanold) publish *some* searches; most do not.
- The signal-rich source is **LinkedIn job postings** (where companies post, sometimes with retained-firm partner) + **named recruiter posts** ("we're representing a Series B CRO search").
- **Direct DM to a named recruiter is the higher-leverage play than scraping firm pages.** Section C is where the value is.

Also: I cannot access LinkedIn poster names without authentication; LinkedIn's anonymous job feed strips recruiter attribution. Where I list a LinkedIn job, the apply path is the LinkedIn link itself; if the role is retained-rep'd, the recruiter surfaces post-application.
