## Section B — DM Cadence

- **Volume:** 5 DMs per week
- **Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday only (mornings, before 10am PT)
- **No follow-ups** for 14 days after first DM. If they reply, respond same day. If silence by day 14, send one polite follow-up; if silence by day 28, mark dormant and move on.
- **Tracking:** CRM-style spreadsheet — Notion, Airtable, or Google Sheet — one row per firm. Columns mirror Section A table plus "Reply received" and "Follow-up sent."
- **Pacing math:** 5/week × 5 weeks ≈ 25 DMs sent across the full Section A list. Do not exceed 5/week — LinkedIn flags burst sending.

## Section D — Verification Checklist (per recruiter, pre-send)

Run all four steps before pressing send. If any step fails, do not send.

1. **Confirm recruiter is still at that firm.** Open their LinkedIn profile, check the Experience section. Current role must list the firm with no end date. If they moved firms, update the Section A row to the new firm or skip.
2. **Confirm altitude match.** Skim their last 5 posts and their Experience bullets for evidence they place CRO / Chief Revenue Officer / Founding Head of Sales / VP Sales (Series A-C). If their book is SDR / AE / mid-management only, skip — wrong altitude.
3. **Recent-post personalization.** Skim their Activity tab, last 30 days. Pull one concrete signal: a search they posted about, a market take they shared, a portfolio company they named. This becomes the `[specialty / portfolio area]` line. If they have zero posts in 30 days, use a generic firm-level reference instead (e.g., "Daversa's Series B CRO work").
4. **Personalize and send.** Paste DM template, fill all three variables, re-read once for choogy, send Tuesday-Thursday morning.
