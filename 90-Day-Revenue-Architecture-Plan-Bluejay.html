90 Days to a Working Sales Engine

Phase 1: Days 1-30
Take Over Inbound, Launch Outbound, Close
✓ Take ownership of inbound. The CEO is currently running inbound channels. Take them over immediately. Qualify, follow up, and convert inbound leads faster. Free the CEO to focus on product and strategic accounts.
✓ Architect outbound. Design multi-channel outbound: email, LinkedIn, events, enterprise segmentation. Build it like a product: test, measure, refine. Target companies deploying voice AI agents at scale who have no evaluation infrastructure.
✓ Close deals. Own the full sales cycle from first touch to signed contract. Run discovery with product and engineering leaders deploying voice AI. Build the trust case: simulation testing, adversarial red teaming, compliance validation. Navigate enterprise procurement.
✓ GTM engineering. Build lead lists using enrichment, automation, and AI-native tools. Create semi-automated workflows that improve pipeline velocity. Obsess over signal: what is working, what is not, and why.
Phase 2: Days 31-60
Content, Partnerships, Enterprise Expansion
✓ Content and brand. Ship consistent content across LinkedIn, written channels, and the voice AI ecosystem. Turn product wins into distribution leverage. Improve discoverability through SEO and AI-native distribution. Position Bluejay as the authority on trustworthy AI agent testing.
✓ Ecosystem partnerships. Build relationships across the voice AI ecosystem: agent platforms, contact center providers, compliance consultants. Coordinate co-marketing initiatives. Increase credibility and trust in enterprise markets through partner validation.
✓ Enterprise deal execution. Push Fortune 500 opportunities through complex procurement. Navigate security reviews, compliance requirements, and multi-stakeholder approval. Build the enterprise closing motion.
✓ Events and in-person. Work conferences, dinners, and in-person events alongside founders. Build the relationships that drive enterprise pipeline in a trust-driven market. Every handshake is a potential six-figure deal.
Phase 3: Days 61-90
Growth Engine Running
✓ Revenue accelerating. Multiple channels producing pipeline: outbound, inbound, partnerships, content, events. Revenue trajectory continuing the doubling pattern. The growth engine is built and running.
✓ Playbook documented. The complete GTM system documented as a product: ICP definitions, outbound sequences, inbound conversion process, content strategy, partnership playbook, enterprise sales process. Ready for the next hire to execute.
✓ Category authority established. Bluejay is the name people say when they say "AI agent testing." Content, customer stories, and ecosystem presence all reinforce the positioning: trust is not a feature, it is the foundation.
✓ Scaling plan defined. Based on 90 days of real data, the growth roadmap is clear: which channels to invest in, which segments to expand into, what the team needs to look like. Ready to hire and scale.
